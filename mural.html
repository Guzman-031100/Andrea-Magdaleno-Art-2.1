Hometown Public Library

This mural was a volunteer opportunity to showcase my skills as an artist and to
give back to my community. This project gave me the chance to get to know the
people of Hometown and to work alongside Hometown Public Library.
Throughout the process of making, there were challenges that I was faced with
as an artist. It was also an opportunity to have fun and meet new people!
